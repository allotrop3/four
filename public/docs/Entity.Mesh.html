Class: Mesh

Entity. Mesh

new Mesh(nameopt, vao, verticesopt, colorsopt, uvsopt, normalsopt, indicesopt, usageopt, material)

A mesh is a collection of vertices and faces that define the construction of a shape. It further includes data to shade the mesh, such as vertex colors, texture mapping coordinates and per-vertex/face normals.
