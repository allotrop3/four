Entity. VertexArrayObject

new VertexArrayObject(nameopt, indexedopt, viewopt, attributesopt)

VertexArrayObject retains the attributes associated with the rendering for an associated mesh, therefore providing a handler to manage their referencing in the vertex shader at render time
