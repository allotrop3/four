Class: Structure

Entity. Structure

new Structure(nameopt, program, path, uniformsopt)

A structure, at a high-level, is a concept that groups a collection of related uniforms and their values. Alternatively, it is an abstraction layer to link client-side properties with their uniform counter-part, thereby simplifying the process of updating uniforms on the shader. Extensions, however, may introduce functionality bespoke to a particular concept.
